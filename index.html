How to Buy

Step 1: Create a wallet

We recommend to download the Metamask wallet from the app store or google play. If you are a desktop user then download the Google Chrome extension through the official Metamask website. You can also try other Solana compatible wallets

Step 2: Get ETH balance

To be able to buy you will need ETH in your Metamask wallet to swap it for $WIZARD tokens. You can buy ETH directly on Metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy it on an exchange and send it to your DeFi wallet.

Step 3: Go to Raydium

Open Raydium. Connect the wallet that you funded. Make sure to paste the official $WIZARD token address that is stated on the website, and confirm. Carefully follow all further instructions inside your wallet.

Step 4: Swap for $WIZARD

Swap your ETH for $WIZARD. After you follow further instructions in your Metamask wallet and have confirmed the transaction then you are a new holder of $WIZARD! Lets go to the moon together.
